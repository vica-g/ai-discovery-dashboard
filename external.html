About Leland's AI Builder Program

Leland helps non-technical professionals and teams learn to build with AI through cohort-based programs. The pattern that works best — by far — is bringing teams, leadership groups, or executive cohorts through together.

When everyone learns to build at the same time, the curve compounds: AI champions no longer have to bear the burden of being the go-to educator, enabler, and troubleshooter for the org's AI experiments. Everyone is on the same level, using the same vocabulary, and capable of solving most snafus alone.
